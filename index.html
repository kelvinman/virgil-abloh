"Virgil Abloh"

1980

Virgil Abloh was born.

2002

Obtained his bachelor degree in civil engineering from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

2009

Founded RSVP Gallery, an art gallery and menswear boutique in Chicago.

2011

Art director for the Jay-Z / Kanye West album Watch the Throne, for which he was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Recording Package.

2012

Launched his first fashion brand, Pyrex Vision, which screen-printed logos onto Champion t-shirts and dead stock Ralph Lauren rugby shirts.

2014

Launched the Milan-based fashion label Off-White in offering high-end men's and women's collections.
